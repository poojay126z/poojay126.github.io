My Story

A Journey of
Three Homes

Born and raised in the warmth of Gujarat, I carry the vibrant colours of my roots everywhere I go — in my cooking, my love for celebrations, and my deep faith.

Life took me to Pune, where the rains and the streets taught me a new rhythm of life. Every phase has added a new layer to who I am.

Today, I call Michigan, USA home — alongside my wonderful husband Ajay and our little star, Kridha. Far from Gujarat, but Gujarat never leaves the heart.

I am a homemaker in the truest, fullest sense — and I wear that with pride. The house I tend is filled with laughter, the smell of good food, music, and the tiny footsteps of a curious girl growing too fast.

🌅

Gujarat, India

The beginning — where festivals are grand, food is spectacular, and family is everything. Home of garba nights, kite festivals, and the strongest chai you'll ever taste.

🌿

Pune, India

A city of energy and culture. New friends, new experiences, and a growing sense of self — Pune was an adventure that shaped many of her best memories.

❄️

Michigan, USA

Snow, seasons, and a whole new world to explore. Building a home abroad with Ajay and Kridha — bringing Gujarat's warmth to America's Midwest.

Faith & Devotion

Jai Shri Ram —
Devotee of Hanuman Ji

Among all of Pooja's loves, her devotion to Lord Hanuman holds a special place. In the great Veer — the symbol of strength, loyalty, and selfless service — she finds both comfort and courage.

Whether it's the Hanuman Chalisa in the morning, the small murti on the home altar, or a quiet prayer before a big day, her faith is woven into the fabric of everyday life. It grounds her, guides her, and reminds her what truly matters.
